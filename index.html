Common Questions

Is a driver included with the rental? +
Yes, all our vehicles can be booked with a professional driver included. Self-drive options are available for select vehicles on request — contact us to discuss.
How far in advance should I book? +
We recommend booking at least 24 hours in advance to secure your preferred vehicle. For weddings or peak season dates, 3–5 days notice is ideal. Same-day bookings are accepted subject to availability.
Are fuel costs included in the price? +
Fuel is generally the customer's responsibility unless a package price is agreed upon. We will clarify this at the time of booking so there are no surprises.
Can I book for outstation travel from Multan? +
Absolutely. We provide outstation travel across Punjab and Sindh. Rates for long-distance trips are calculated separately — call or WhatsApp us with your destination for a custom quote.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept cash, bank transfer (online payment), and Easypaisa / JazzCash mobile payments. A partial advance may be required at time of booking for premium vehicles.
